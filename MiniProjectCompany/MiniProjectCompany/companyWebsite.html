Startup to open a restaurant business

Nowadays, we need to take into consideration many different aspects to start a restaurant business.
In the restaurant field, it is key to understand the needs of the customer in order to make him not only try out your restaurant but have him as repeated customer. Customers have different taste,needs and values when shopping for a meal.

In order to help the owner of the restaurant keep his business healthy and up to date, we decided to create this application to let him analyze his market in order to reach potential and satisfy his customers and best.



Analyze different user types


Customers have several needs when shopping for a meal, some look for pricing, some for quantities and others for qualities. It is important to analyze your customers needs in order to make your sell, a recursive sell. Remember, as Jihm Rohn once said, f you make a sale, you can make a living. If you make an investment of time and good service in a customer, you can make a fortune. This is why we need to undertand users needs, experiences, behaviors and goals.
